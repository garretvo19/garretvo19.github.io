I am an industrial engineering PhD student at Florida State University (FSU) working with Professor Chiwoo Park in Data Mining Research Laboratory (Data Mining). My research interest is in developing algorithms to detect and track the movement of nano-objects in electron microscopic images. In addition to computer vision, I also did research and developed algorithms in Bayesian nonparametric statistics, compressed sensing, and spatial statistics. During my time at FSU, I have interned at Humana (Humana) as a data scientist and worked on text mining.

Before FSU, I received my Bachelor of Science in Physics and Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering at Montana State University(MSU). During my time at MSU, I applied the Immerse Boundary Method (Immerse Boundary) to study fluid-structure interaction in biofilm, and the Discontinuous Galerkin Method (DGM) to solve parabolic partial differential equations.
